# Short-Form Video Hooks (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)

10 scripts, 15-30 seconds each. Format optimized for vertical video, captioned-on, sound-off-friendly.

**Production notes:**
- Hook in the first 1.5 seconds — text on screen IMMEDIATELY, no intro card.
- Caption every word burned-in. 80%+ of users watch with sound off.
- End card 2 seconds: "NFTones — Own the Sound. Link in bio."
- Founder face-to-camera works better than B-roll for hooks 1, 5, 10.
- Daily cadence > production polish. Ship 1/day.
