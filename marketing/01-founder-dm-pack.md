# Founder DM Pack

5 cold DM templates by audience. Tuned to the master-directive language rules: artist-ownership framing, no speculation language, no "guaranteed" anything, no $KTRS-purchase gating. Each template stays under ~600 characters so it fits in DM constraints across X / Instagram / TikTok / Discord.

**Customization checklist before sending:**
- Swap `[name]`, `[track]`, `[their-thing]` for real specifics. Generic = ignored.
- Listen to one of their songs / look at one of their posts first. Reference it in the opener.
- Keep the "Yo" opener for hip-hop/producer/underground audiences. Swap to "Hey" for label / vocalist / collective audiences.
- End every DM with a low-friction ask ("down to take a look", "10-min walkthrough"), never "buy" or "invest".

## Reply playbook

When they ask "what's the catch?":
> No catch. Free to upload, free drop pages. Mint only happens when a fan buys, and the cost comes out of the sale or the buyer covers it — that's how I keep it free without going broke. Premium tools (AI mastering, promo, boosts, analytics) run on $KTRS credits, but you don't need any of that to start.

When they ask "what's $KTRS?":
> $KTRS is the credit you spend on premium creator tools — AI mastering, cover art, promo copy, boosts, advanced analytics. Not an investment, not something you have to buy to start. You can earn it through referrals and platform activity, or buy it when you want to power up.

When they ask "is this just NFTs?":
> No. NFTones is artist ownership infrastructure. The on-chain piece exists so you actually own your masters and your royalty splits route automatically. The product itself is a music business platform — uploads, drop pages, fan access, royalty tools. The blockchain part stays out of your way.

When they ask "what about copyright?":
> NFTones gives you on-chain timestamping, rights declarations, and royalty routing tools. It doesn't replace formal copyright registration — for that you still want to register with the copyright office. We make it easier to prove what you released and when, and to route the money correctly.
